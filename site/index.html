The feed is designed to win.

Hours disappear into a phone. Then the money that is easy to reach disappears too.

Screen-time limits usually ask for willpower in the exact moment attention is weakest. TouchGrass moves the decision upstream: choose a budget while you are clear-minded, then earn back access to a small daily allowance by protecting your time.

Not punishment.
A promise you can see.

01

Plant a budget

Mint harmless demo mUSDC, choose a 7, 14, or 28-day program, and lock the whole allowance upfront.

02

Live your actual day

Android aggregates foreground app time while excluding TouchGrass itself. Per-app activity never leaves the phone.

03

Claim what you earned

After a successful completed day, a verifier signs a one-time voucher. The Monad vault releases that day’s mUSDC.

04

Let misses become savings

Go over your limit? Nothing is burned. That day’s mUSDC remains locked until the program and cooldown end.
